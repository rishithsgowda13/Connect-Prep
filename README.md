# Connect & Prep 📚

**Connect & Prep** is a student-centric academic platform designed to solve the everyday challenges faced by engineering students.

---

## ❓ The Problem

Students often struggle with:
- **Scattered Resources**: Notes, question papers, and materials are spread across WhatsApp groups, drives, and random folders.
- **Ineffective Exam Prep**: No central place to find previous year questions (PYQs) and understand what topics are frequently asked.
- **Isolation**: Studying alone without peer support or mentorship from seniors and alumni.
- **Manual Tracking**: Lack of a single dashboard to manage attendance, results, and academic deadlines.

## ✅ The Solution

**Connect & Prep** brings everything together in one modern, easy-to-use platform:

1.  **Question Paper Hub**: A database of previous year questions, organized by subject, with "Years Asked" analysis to identify important topics.
2.  **Notes Repository**: Access high-quality notes curated by teachers and verified top students.
3.  **Collaborative Tools**: Group Study rooms, Peer-to-Peer (P2P) Tutoring, and a Doubt Solving forum.
4.  **Productivity Features**: Study Marathons (Pomodoro-style timers), AI-powered result analysis, and attendance tracking.
5.  **Library & More**: Check book availability and connect with alumni.
